Our Services

Cutting Routing Laser Work Gluing Cementing Polishing

Cutting

Our precision cutting services ensure clean, accurate cuts for acrylic sheets of any size or thickness, using state-of-the-art equipment to meet your exact specifications. Whether you need straight cuts, intricate patterns, or complex shapes, our expert team can handle all your acrylic cutting needs with impeccable precision and efficiency.

Routing

Our advanced routing services provide detailed and intricate designs on acrylic sheets, perfect for creating custom patterns, shapes, and decorative elements. Using high-precision CNC routers, we deliver consistent and accurate results for all your acrylic routing projects, ensuring a flawless finish every time.

Laser Work

Experience unparalleled accuracy and detail with our laser cutting services, ideal for creating intricate designs, precise cuts, and clean edges on acrylic sheets. Our laser technology allows for high-speed, efficient cutting and engraving of acrylic, making it perfect for both small-scale projects and large production runs.

Gluing/Cementing

Our professional gluing and cementing services offer strong, seamless bonds for acrylic components, ensuring durable and aesthetically pleasing results. Using specialized adhesives and techniques, we provide reliable and transparent joints that maintain the clarity and integrity of your acrylic projects.

Polishing

Restore the clarity and shine of your acrylic sheets with our expert polishing services, which remove scratches, blemishes, and dullness for a flawless finish. Our polishing process enhances the visual appeal of your acrylic products, delivering a smooth, glossy surface that looks as good as new.

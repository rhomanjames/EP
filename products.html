Acrylic Sheets

Acrylic sheets are versatile, durable, and transparent materials used in a variety of applications, from signage and glazing to furniture and decorative accents. Available in a range of colors, finishes, and thicknesses, acrylic sheets provide a cost-effective and lightweight solution for both indoor and outdoor projects.

  • Clear
  • Opaque
  • Transparent
  • Frame Grade
  • OP3
  • Museum Grade

Mirrored Acrylic

Mirrored acrylic offers a sleek and modern alternative to traditional glass mirrors, combining lightweight durability with a high-gloss, reflective surface. Ideal for decorative and functional applications, mirrored acrylic is perfect for creating stylish displays, adding visual depth, and enhancing lighting effects in any space.

  • Silver
  • Gold
  • Rose Gold
  • Pink
  • Purple
  • Orange

Textures

Textured acrylic sheets add a tactile dimension to your projects, offering a variety of finishes such as frosted, patterned, or embossed to enhance visual interest and functionality. These textured surfaces can diffuse light, provide privacy, or create unique aesthetic effects, making them suitable for both decorative and practical applications.

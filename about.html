About Us

We are dedicated to quality and excellence in the services we provide and the products we produce.

Who We Are

Everything Plastic is the one-stop shop for all of your plastic needs! Formerly Arch Street Plastics and Plastics of Phliadelphia, Everything Plastic has been serving the greater Philly area for decades! From stocked plastics like trophy cases and awards to custom fabrication, we offer whatever it is you may need. We also ship worldwide, right from our retail store and fabrication shop in the Philadelphia metro area.

Our Mission

At Everything Plastic, our mission is to bring your vision to life. Whether you’re in the Philly metro area or overseas, we’ll work with you to craft the perfect custom pieces for your event, home, office or business.
